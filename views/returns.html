Returns & Exchanges

Returns are free

Lyla really loves it when you fall in love with her products, but she understands that there will be times when you might want to return something. To get a full refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Email returns@lylaloves.co.uk and let us know:
  • Your Order Number
  • Your reason for returning the item
  • Whether you want an exchange or refund

We will then email you a return label that will ensure that you can return the item(s) for free.

  1. Return to the item to us within 14 days of placing the order to the following address:

LYLA LOVES

Unit 11 Adrienne Avenue

Adrienne Business Centre

Southall Middlesex

UB1 2FJ

We will email you confirmation that we have received your return and process the refund on the next working day.

A Couple More Points

  1. For hygiene reasons, we cannot refund earrings or ear cuffs unless they are damaged or faulty.
  1. Make sure the items are in the same condition as they were when you received them. We will be unable to issue you a refund if they are altered in any way.

None of the above effects your statutory rights.
